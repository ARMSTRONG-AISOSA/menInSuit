About Event

This event, themed MEN IN S.U.I.T. is designed specifically for young men who are Saved, Unmarried, Intentional, and Thriving. It is an intimate gathering of a few like-minded individuals passionate about growth and accountability in the key areas of life;

  • Faith
  • Family
  • Finance
  • Fitness (Health)

About Host

Victory Benjamin is a self-driven individual passionate about leading and influencing a highly effective life through Leadership and human capacity development. He is currently the founder and team lead of Youlead_Africa, a social impact group with a vision to inspire and empower African youths for global impact through several coaching and training that reposition the minds of young people for a quality and effective life. Victory Benjamin is a public speaker and a preacher of the Gospel of Christ leading young people to the place of fulfilment in life.

Our Schedule

Do not miss anything about the event

Heart 2 Heart Session

  • This session is the heart of the evening. It offers an open and honest environment for you to share your experiences, challenges, wisdom and victories. It’s a time and place where young men can openly discuss personal struggles, triumphs, ambition, faith and areas for growth.
  • This session will emphasize the power of vulnerability, accountability, and support within a community of like-minded men.

Networking Session

  • The Networking Session provides an opportunity for you to build connections with other growth-minded individuals. Here you can meet peers who share your values of intentionality and success, exchange ideas, share experiences, and set up collaborations that can contribute to personal and professional growth.
  • This time is dedicated to forging meaningful bonds that extend beyond the event.

Fine Dinning

  • Throughout the evening, guests will enjoy a premium fine dining experience. As discussions and networking unfold, the table will be filled with an assortment of delicious, thoughtfully prepared meals. This shared dining experience adds an element of elegance and community to the event as participants bond over excellent cuisine.
  • it’s a celebration of brotherhood and shared purpose.
